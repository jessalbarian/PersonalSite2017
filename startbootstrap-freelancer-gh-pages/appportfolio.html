What Lifts You iOS App

I created from the ground-up an iOS mobile app using Apple's development language, Swift. The app is for international street artist, Kelsey Montague, and it allows users to place illustrations of wings on photos which are then animated for sharing to social media. The app is currently in the App Store for iPhone 10.0 and later.

What Lifts You App
  • Skills used: Firebase, Swift, iOS development, Xcode, Illustrator, custom graphics, submitting an app to the App Store

CU Boulder Mobile App

I worked with one of my colleagues to create an app for the University of Colorado Boulder. We collected tons of data such as parking information, building locations, and dining information with the goal of being able to help students and people visiting the campus. This project is still currently being developed, so it is not yet in the App Store.

  • Skills used: Firebase, Swift, iOS development, Xcode, Illustrator, custom graphics, data collection and storage

Patches Mobile App

I created an iOS and Android app called Patches that showed animals available for adoption at certain animal shelters and humane societies in the Colorado area. This was a school project in the Advanced Mobile App Development class at the University of Colorado Boulder, and I built the Android version at a hackathon.

  • Skills used: Firebase, Swift, iOS development, Xcode, Java, Android Studio, Android development, Illustrator, custom graphics, web server setup, Python scripts, cronjobs

BlueLight Mobile App

I created an iOS app called BlueLight that was aimed at replacing the blue emergency polls at universities. It took the user's location, the app stayed on once opened, and when you tapped the large blue button in the middle, it would either call campus security or 911 based on the user selection. I wanted to make this app for my university, CU Boulder, to make people feel safer on campus. You can see the code on my GitHub.

iOS App on GitHub
  • Skills used: Swift, iOS development, Xcode, Illustrator, custom graphics

SafeSpot Mobile App

I created an iOS app called SafeSpot at a hackathon with a team of 3 other people. The goal of SafeSpot was to show people where "safe spots" were. A safe spot is a business who accepts anyone from any race, gender, ethnicity, sexual orientation, etc. Companies would have to register to become a "SafeSpot" and it would show up on a map. You can see the code on my GitHub.

iOS App on DevPost
  • Skills used: Swift, iOS development, Xcode, Illustrator, custom graphics, MapQuest API, location services
