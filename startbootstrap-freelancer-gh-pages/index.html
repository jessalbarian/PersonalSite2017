Hi! I'm Jessie.

I am a professional freelance software developer and designer who has experience with websites, apps, and UX/UI. But I don’t just build products, I create solutions for companies. I dive deep into my client’s goals, needs, and wants, and my work revolves around these in order to help the overall success of their business.

I am an artist at heart with a logical mind, so I work on projects with both my creativity and problem solving mindset. This is where the "half developer - half designer" comes from.

When I am not working, you can find me reading a book in my hammock, writing down random business ideas in my journal, or hiking trails across the Rocky Mountains!
